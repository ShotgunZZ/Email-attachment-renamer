Gmail Attachment Renamer Options

Preview: 2023-12-31_user@example.com_document.pdf
Customize how your downloaded attachments will be renamed.

Available Variables:

Use these variables in your pattern:

  • YYYY-MM-DD - Email date (selected format above)
  • SenderEmail - Email address of the sender
  • SenderName - Name of the email sender (if available)
  • OriginalFilename - Original attachment filename
  • Subject - Email subject (truncated if too long)

Example: YYYY-MM-DD_SenderEmail_OriginalFilename

All special characters will be automatically sanitized and replaced with underscores.
