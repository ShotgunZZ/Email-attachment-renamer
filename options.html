Gmail Attachment Renamer Options

Customize how your downloaded attachments will be renamed.

Available Variables:

Use these variables in your pattern:

  • YYYY-MM-DD - Email date (Year-Month-Day)
  • SenderName - Name of the email sender
  • OriginalFilename - Original attachment filename

Example: YYYY-MM-DD_SenderName_OriginalFilename

All special characters will be automatically sanitized and replaced with underscores.
